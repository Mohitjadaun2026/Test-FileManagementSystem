## 📞 Quick Support

### Common Questions

**Q: Where is the profile menu?**  
A: Click the circular avatar image in the top-right corner of the navbar

**Q: How do I edit my profile?**  
A: Click "View Profile" → Click "Edit Profile" → Make changes → Click "Save Changes"

**Q: What can I change?**  
A: Your profile picture and name. Email cannot be changed.

**Q: Where are my statistics from?**  
A: Real-time data from the backend, based on files you've uploaded

**Q: Do my changes save automatically?**  
A: No, you must click "Save Changes" to persist them

**Q: Where are images stored?**  
A: In your browser's localStorage (safe and private)

**Q: Can I access my profile on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The profile page is fully responsive
